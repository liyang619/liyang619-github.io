I am a doctoral student in Computer Science at the University of Manchester specializing, with a particular emphasis on multi-agent learning and applications of AI. I am supervised by Dr. Wei Pan and co-supervised by Prof. Angelo Cangelosi. Before starting PhD journey, I obtained the Master degree in Information and Communication Engineering from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences under Prof. Jun Wang and Prof. Yang Yang, and the Bachelor degree in Digital Media Technology from Dalian University of Technology.

My recent research focuses on multi-agent learning in open-ended worlds, aiming to improve the adaptability of multi-agent systems to varying tasks, emergent on-the-fly co-players, and other evolving environments. The ultimate aim of my research is to advance multi-agent interaction in real-world applications where unpredictability is the norm.
